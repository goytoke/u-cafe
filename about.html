Our Mission

Our mission at U Cafe is to create better days for everyone, every day. Something as simple as a cup of coffee has the power to transform your everyday into something special, so we create better days one brewed-to-order cup at a time. We approach every interaction with warmth, sincerity, and care, to raise each other's spirits (while raising a cup).

The Story of U Cafe

The Challenge & The Idea

The story of U Cafe begins in Addis Ababa, at Ayer Amba Secondary School. The school cafeteria faced several challenges: pricing issues, limited menu options, inconsistent quality, and outdated payment methods. Recognizing these problems, a group of innovative Grade 11 Natural Science students from Room 01 decided to take action. Their vision was to create a modern solution that would transform the entire cafeteria experience.

The Solution: U Online Cafe

From this vision, U Online Cafe was born. It is a modern online platform designed to improve every aspect of the school cafeteria. With U Online Cafe, the menu now offers a wider selection of quality items, and the payment system is fully cashless and convenient.

The platform primarily serves teachers, staff, and the surrounding school community. Recognizing that students have limited access to devices, a special arrangement was made with the school director to allow students to place orders using the school's IT lab computers, ensuring everyone can benefit from this initiative.
